Le Vilain Petit Canard

Il y a un petit canard qui est différent des autres.
There is a little duckling who is different from the others.
Les autres canetons se moquent de lui parce qu’il est laid.
The other ducklings laugh at him because he is ugly.
Le petit canard se sent triste et quitte le lac.
The duckling feels sad and leaves the lake.
Il marche seul et il a très froid pendant l’hiver.
He walks alone and feels very cold in the winter.
Quand le printemps arrive, il voit des cygnes sur le lac.
When spring comes, he sees some swans on the lake.
Il regarde l’eau et voit son reflet : maintenant, c’est un beau cygne.
He looks in the water and sees his reflection: now he is a beautiful swan.
Le vilain petit canard est heureux et tout le monde l’aime.
The ugly duckling is happy and everyone likes him.
